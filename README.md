\# TowerBuilder



Basic Mechanics: 

\- The slope will balance from one way to another in a pendular motion. Observe the rythm and click!



\- Pile one slope into another, but be careful! They may fall! Compete to beat your own highscore. 



\- If you align your slope with the one below, they will snap into place granting you 250 points! Normal landing blocks only grant 100 points.



If a slope or the entire tower falls, its game over!
